About the Book

Your unfair advantage
starts here.

College is the best time to start a company. You have access to mentors, low living costs, a built-in network, and the freedom to take risks. But most students don't know where to begin.

After working with hundreds of student entrepreneurs as Davidson's Innovator-in-Residence, I kept hearing the same questions: How do I find an idea? How do I find the time? How do I get customers? How do I fundraise?

This book distills those conversations into a practical, step-by-step manual for students ready to build something real. You have nothing to lose and everything to build.
